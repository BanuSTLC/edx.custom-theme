For Course-on-Course Subscriptions



Ace will not allow to do refunds for the payments that has been made on a Course-on-Course subscription plan. Learners must cancel the subscription before their fee trails end in order to avoid getting charged when free trial promotions are taken place. If a learner completes a course during the free trial period, Ace hold the right to require the student to pay the course fee in order to issue the specialization certificate or/and to proceed with another course. Your subscription will remain on a course on course basis until you cancel or subscription is suspended or discontinued by Ace. Cancellation will be effective at the end of the current monthly period; you will continue to have access to your subscription for the remainder of that period, and no refund will be given in such a situation.



Learners are requested to read the course description, module structure, learning outcomes to make sure that course meets their learning objectives.



However if a course is been cancelled or rescheduled due to quality review or any other unavoidable circumstances , Ace University will allocate the funds to be used in a new course with same or less value. In the case that the initial investment is lower than the new course that the learners wish to take, learners will have to pay the remaining course fee after deduction of the previous course fee.
